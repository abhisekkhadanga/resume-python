Journey So Far...

As luffy's father, Monkey D. Dragon, is one of the most wanted man by World Goverment, he was left with his Grand Father Monkey D. Garp. Liffy grew up in Foosha village and met with Shanks when he was only 6. Not only Shanks was fond of luffy, he was also impressed by him to the point that he gave him his hat. Luffy also ate the Gomu Gomu Devil Fruit at that time. After the incindent with Shanks, which I won't go any deeper, Garp moved Luffy to Mt. Colubo to stay with Dadan Family where he met with Ace and later Sabo. Three of them trained together and became sworn brothers. Later they got separated to go in their own ways.

At the age of 17 luffy started his journey to become the new Pirate King. He first met with Zoro in Shells Town and recruited him. Then he met with Nami in Loguetown first, but Nami was recruited at Arlong Park after luffy defeated Arlong. Usopp was recruited at his hometown Syrup Village. Then he went on to recuit Sanji in Baratie Ship. Rest of the crew recruitement happened in Grand Line. Chopper was recruited from Drum Islands. Similar to Nami, Luffy first met with Robin in Baroque works arc and Robin became permanent membre at the end of Alabasta arc after luffy defeated Crocodile. Franky was first introduced in Water 7 arc. He joined the crew at the end of Enies Lobby arc. Franky also constructed Thousand Sunny after Going Mary was destroyed. Brook was floating in Florian Triangle when he first met with Luffy. Luffy defeated Moria to bring back his shadow and he joined Straw Hat Pirates. Lastly Jinbed was introduced properly to the audinece in Fish-Man islands. His relation with Straw Hat crew was very complex and different fromothers. From being a Sichibukai he became subordinate of Big Mom after Whitebeard was defeated. But after meeting with Luffy agreed to join his crew, which he did in Whole Cake islands Arc.

Together Mugibara Pirates have given the one piece many surprises. Their victory over Crocodile, Enel and Moria were some of the remarkable battles before timeskip. They wages war against World Goverment in Enies Lobby Arc and defeated CP9 in that saga as well. After timeskip their fight in Dressrosa, Whole Cake Islands and now in Wano is bringing them fame.
